## Summary

candle-gen is a Rust-based image generation framework built on top of the Candle ML library, implementing diffusion model pipelines. The candle-gen-chroma subproject specifically implements the Chroma diffusion model in three variants (base, flash, HD), including noise scheduling with beta distribution-based sigma schedules, model configuration, and image generation pipelines.
